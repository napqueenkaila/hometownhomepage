Top three activities to do in Michigan

Ann Arbor

Cheer on the University of Michigan Wolverines at the Big House, the largest stadium in the U.S.!

Mackinac Island

Rent a bike or carriage to tour the island then head downtown for some world-famous Mackinac Island Fudge!

Upper Peninsula

Appreciate breath-taking views of Pictured Rocks, Taquamenon Falls and more!
