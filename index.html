Top three places to go in Michigan

Mackinac Island

Rent a bike or carriage to tour the island then head downtown for some world-famous Mackinac Island Fudge!

Ann Arbor

Come in the fall to cheer on the University of Michigan Wolverines at the Big House, the largest stadium in the U.S.!

Downtown Detroit

Head to the Motor City for a night out! With great concert venues and a variety of delicious cusines you'll be in for a treat!
